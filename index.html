Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is included in the yacht rental price?

The rental includes the yacht, professional captain and crew, fuel for the agreed route, insurance, and basic amenities. Additional services like food, beverages, and decorations are available at extra cost.

What is the minimum booking duration?

Minimum booking is 2 hours for standard yachts and 3 hours for luxury vessels. We recommend at least 2 hours to properly enjoy Dubai's coastline.

Can we customize our route?

Absolutely! While we offer popular routes, you can customize your itinerary. Our captains know the best spots and can suggest routes based on your preferences.

What happens in case of bad weather?

Safety is our priority. If weather conditions are unsafe, you can reschedule or receive a full refund. We monitor weather closely and will inform you in advance.
